# Sun Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# These appear when shining light on a completed sprout - focus on macro planning,
# organization, looking backward and forward, strategic thinking.

# Looking Back - What Happened
What was the arc of this journey?
How did this unfold compared to what you expected?
What were the major turning points?
Map out the phases this went through.
What story does this goal tell?

# Looking Back - Lessons Learned
What did this teach you about yourself?
What patterns from your past showed up again?
What would you tell someone starting this same goal?
What skills did you develop through this?
What assumptions were wrong?

# Looking Back - Honest Assessment
Did you give this the attention it deserved?
What would you do differently with hindsight?
Where did you compromise too much?
What did you learn about your limits?
Were you the person you wanted to be during this?

# The Bigger Picture
How does this fit into your life's direction?
What chapter of your story is this?
How did this change your trajectory?
What does completing this make possible?
Where does this sit among your priorities?

# Future Planning - What's Next
What naturally follows from this?
What new goals emerged from this experience?
How should this influence your next decisions?
What unfinished threads want attention?
What door did this open that you want to walk through?

# Future Planning - Systems & Habits
What systems could support goals like this?
What habit would make similar goals easier?
How could you organize your life to do more of this?
What structure was missing that you need?
What recurring practice would help?

# Future Planning - Resource Allocation
How should you invest your time differently going forward?
What should you do more of? Less of?
Who should you spend more time with?
What tools or resources do you need?
Where should your energy go next?

# Integration & Identity
How did this change how you see yourself?
What kind of person completes goals like this?
What does this say about who you're becoming?
How does this connect to your values?
What part of you grew through this?

# Gratitude & Closure
What are you most grateful for from this journey?
Who deserves acknowledgment for helping?
What moments do you want to remember?
How do you want to honor this completion?
What do you want to carry forward?

# Continuity & Grafting
Why might this deserve a second chapter?
What evolved understanding would you bring to a retry?
How has your vision for this matured?
What would "version 2" of this goal look like?
Is there a deeper layer to explore here?

# Release & Letting Go
What are you ready to release?
What no longer serves you from this experience?
What beliefs can you let go of now?
How can you close this chapter with peace?
What space does completing this create?

# Strategic Reflection
What type of goals suit you best?
What conditions help you succeed?
What season of life are you in?
How should this inform your goal-setting approach?
What did this reveal about your capacity?
