# Sun Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# Focus: reflection on the completed goal - past journey, what's next.

# The Journey
How did this actually unfold?
What was the arc of this goal?
What phases did this go through?
How did this change along the way?
What story does this tell?

# What You Learned
What did this teach you?
What skills did you build?
What do you know now that you didn't before?
What surprised you?
What assumptions were wrong?

# Honest Reflection
Did you give this what it deserved?
What would you do differently?
Where did you cut corners?
What did this reveal about your limits?
Are you proud of how you handled this?

# The Outcome
Did this turn out how you expected?
What did completing this actually give you?
Was this worth the effort?
How do you feel now that it's done?
What's different because of this?

# What's Next
What does finishing this make possible?
What naturally follows from here?
Did this spark any new ideas?
What unfinished piece wants attention?
Where does this lead?

# Patterns
Have you done something like this before?
What patterns showed up?
How was this similar to past efforts?
What did this confirm about how you work?
What habit helped or hurt?

# Who Helped
Who supported you through this?
Who deserves thanks?
Who would you tell about this?
What role did others play?
Did anyone make this harder?

# Memorable Moments
What moment stands out most?
What was the hardest point?
What was the best moment?
When did this feel most real?
What will you remember?

# Closure
How do you want to close this chapter?
What do you want to carry forward?
Is there anything left unsaid?
What can you let go of now?
How do you want to mark this completion?

# Continuing
Should this have a second chapter?
What would a new version look like?
Is there deeper work here?
What would you bring to a revisit?
Why might this deserve more time?

# Integration
How did this change you?
What part of you grew?
How does this connect to what matters?
What does completing this say about you?
Who are you now that this is done?
