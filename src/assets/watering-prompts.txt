# Watering Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# These appear randomly when watering a sprout, asking about progress.

# Progress & Status
What progress have you made on this today?
How close are you to completing this?
What's the current state of this goal?
Where do you stand with this right now?
What have you accomplished so far?

# Recent Actions
What did you do for this today?
What's one thing you did to move this forward?
What action did you take most recently?
How did you work on this since last time?
What steps have you taken lately?

# Obstacles & Challenges
What's been the hardest part so far?
What obstacles are you facing?
Is anything blocking your progress?
What challenge came up recently?
What's been more difficult than expected?

# Learnings & Insights
What have you learned while working on this?
Any surprises along the way?
What would you do differently so far?
What insight have you gained?
Has your approach changed at all?

# Momentum & Energy
How motivated do you feel about this?
Is this still exciting to work on?
What's keeping you going?
How does this feel right now?
Are you still energized by this goal?

# Next Steps
What's your next move?
What will you focus on next?
What's the immediate next step?
What needs to happen next?
How will you continue tomorrow?

# Reflection
Why does this goal matter to you?
How does this connect to your bigger picture?
What will completing this mean for you?
Who benefits when you succeed at this?
What's at stake here?

# Specifics
Can you describe what "done" looks like?
What does success look like for this?
How will you know when you've made it?
What's the finish line?
What are you actually trying to achieve?

# Time & Pacing
Are you on track with your timeline?
Do you need more or less time than expected?
How does the pacing feel?
Is the deadline realistic?
Should you adjust your expectations?

# Support & Resources
Do you need help with anything?
What resources would make this easier?
Who could support you with this?
Is there something you're missing?
What would accelerate your progress?

# Honesty Check
Are you being honest with yourself about this?
Is this still the right goal?
Should anything change about this goal?
Are you avoiding any part of this?
What are you not admitting to yourself?

# Celebration
What's worth celebrating so far?
What small win happened recently?
What are you proud of?
What went well?
Give yourself credit for something.
