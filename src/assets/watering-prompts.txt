# Watering Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# These appear when watering a growing sprout - focus on the current moment,
# feelings during the process, and actionable next steps.

# How You're Feeling Right Now
How are you feeling about this today?
What emotions came up while working on this?
Does this still feel aligned with what you want?
What's your gut telling you about this?
Are you dreading or looking forward to this?

# Today's Experience
What actually happened today with this?
Describe your session working on this.
What did you notice while doing this?
What moment stands out from today?
How did it feel to work on this?

# Energy & Motivation
Where's your energy level with this right now?
What would make you want to work on this more?
What's draining you about this?
What part of this still excites you?
Is this energizing or depleting you today?

# Obstacles in the Moment
What got in the way today?
What distracted you from this?
What felt harder than it should?
Where did you get stuck?
What resistance showed up?

# Small Wins
What tiny progress did you make?
What's one thing that went right?
What worked better than expected?
What can you feel good about?
Name something you did well today.

# Actionable Next Steps
What's the single next action?
What could you do in the next 10 minutes?
What's the smallest step forward?
If you had 30 minutes tomorrow, what would you do?
What would unblock you right now?

# Honesty in the Moment
Are you avoiding something specific?
What are you procrastinating on?
Is there something you need to admit?
What's the real reason you're stuck?
What would you do if no one was watching?

# Process Reflection
What's working in your approach?
What's not working?
Do you need to try something different?
Is your current method sustainable?
What adjustment would help?

# Emotional Check-in
What do you need right now?
How can you be kinder to yourself about this?
What would make this feel lighter?
Are you being too hard on yourself?
What support would help today?

# Commitment Renewal
Are you still in on this?
What would reconnect you to this goal?
Why did you start this again?
Is this worth your energy today?
What's keeping you going?
