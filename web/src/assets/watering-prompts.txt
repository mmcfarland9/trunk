# Watering Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# Focus: daily check-ins, feelings, actionable steps for the current goal.

# How You're Feeling
How do you feel about this today?
What emotions came up while working on this?
Does this still feel right?
What's your gut saying?
Are you dreading or excited about this?

# Today's Work
What did you actually do today?
Describe your last session working on this.
What happened when you sat down with this?
What moment stands out from today?
How did it feel to spend time on this?

# Energy Check
Where's your energy with this right now?
What would make this feel easier?
What's draining you about this?
What part still excites you?
Is this energizing or depleting you?

# What Got in the Way
What blocked you today?
What distracted you?
Where did you get stuck?
What resistance showed up?
What was harder than expected?

# Small Progress
What tiny step did you take?
What went right today?
What worked?
What can you feel good about?
Name one thing you did well.

# Next Action
What's the single next step?
What could you do in 10 minutes?
What's the smallest move forward?
What would unblock you right now?
If you had 30 minutes tomorrow, what would you do?

# Honesty
Are you avoiding something?
What are you procrastinating on?
What's the real reason you're stuck?
Is there something you need to admit?
What would you do if no one was watching?

# Process
What's working in your approach?
What's not working?
Should you try something different?
Is your method sustainable?
What adjustment would help?

# Self-Compassion
What do you need to keep going?
Are you being too hard on yourself?
What would make this feel lighter?
How can you be kinder to yourself?
What support would help?

# Staying Connected
Are you still committed to this?
What would reconnect you to this goal?
Why did you start this?
Is this worth your energy today?
What's keeping you going?
