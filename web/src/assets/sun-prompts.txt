# Sun Prompts
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are ignored.
# Focus: weekly planning and looking ahead - what's next, how to approach it.

# The Week Ahead
What's your focus for the coming week?
What one thing would make this week successful?
What needs your attention most?
What are you committing to this week?
What's the most important next step?

# Strategy
How will you approach this next phase?
What's your plan of attack?
What obstacles do you expect?
How can you make progress easier?
What would a good week look like?

# Energy & Resources
Do you have what you need to move forward?
What might get in your way?
Where will your energy come from?
What support would help?
How will you protect time for this?

# Intentions
What do you want to feel at the end of the week?
What mindset will serve you?
What are you willing to struggle for?
What would you regret not doing?
How will you stay engaged?

# Adjustments
Does this still matter to you?
Is the approach working?
What needs to change?
Should the scope shift?
What would make this more achievable?

# Environment
Where will you do the work?
When are you at your best?
What distractions need managing?
Who can help hold you accountable?
What conditions help you focus?

# Milestones
What would feel like real progress?
What's the smallest meaningful step?
How will you know you're on track?
What checkpoint would help?
What does "done enough" look like?

# Challenges
What's the hardest part coming up?
What are you avoiding?
Where might you get stuck?
What's the uncomfortable truth?
What would make this easier?

# Motivation
Why does this matter to you?
What's pulling you forward?
What would completing this enable?
Who are you becoming through this?
What's worth the effort?

# This Sprout Specifically
What will you do for this goal this week?
How does this fit with everything else?
Is this the right priority right now?
What's the honest assessment?
What would you tell a friend to do?
